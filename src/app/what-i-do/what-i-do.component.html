8-bit Computer

Software is definitely my strongest area of interest, but I'm interested in hardware as well. As a way to gain a deeper understanding of how a computer works, and to supplement my Digital Logic and Computer Architecture courses, I am building an 8-bit computer on breadboards.

As I learned about latches and comparators, I built a clock for the CPU. As I learned about JK flip flops, I built some registers. I plan to continue to add components as I learn about them.

When I'm feeling crazy, I imagine I might implement TCP/IP on it from scratch. We'll see...
